# #337 「真偽値での表示制御」台本

ずんだもん「Any tips for controlling visibility with booleans?」
四国めたん「Convert Observables or Signals into booleans and pass them to *ngIf for reactive toggles.」
ずんだもん「Do all falsy values hide the element?」
四国めたん「Yes, but 0 or empty strings are also falsy, so make your intent clear in the condition.」
ずんだもん「Leaning on types keeps it safe—very Angular.」
