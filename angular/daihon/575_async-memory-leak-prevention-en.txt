# #575 「メモリリーク防止」台本

ずんだもん「Why does AsyncPipe prevent memory leaks?」
四国めたん「Manual subscriptions may continue after the component is gone, but AsyncPipe's auto-unsubscribe stops the stream when the view disappears.」
ずんだもん「Makes management easier.」
四国めたん「Yes-it saves you from forgetting unsubscribe calls.」
