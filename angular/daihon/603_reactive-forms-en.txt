# #603 「Reactive Forms - リアクティブフォーム」台本

ずんだもん「How do we write a reactive form?」
四国めたん「Compose FormGroup and FormControl instances in TypeScript and bind them to the template.」
ずんだもん「Keeping the logic in TypeScript is a big plus.」
四国めたん「Exactly—you can move validation and state management into services easily.」
ずんだもん「It also seems test-friendly.」
四国めたん「It is—the standard choice for large, complex forms.」
