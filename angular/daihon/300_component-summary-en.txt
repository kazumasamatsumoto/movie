# #300 「Component 総まとめと実践プロジェクト」台本

ずんだもん「What should we keep in mind to wrap up the component chapter?」
四国めたん「Center on separation of concerns, state management, and accessibility, and implement everything in line with the design system.」
ずんだもん「How do we move forward in a project?」
四国めたん「Stabilize foundational components like buttons and form fields first, then pair smart and dumb components per feature.」
ずんだもん「How do we ensure quality?」
四国めたん「Build Storybook, unit tests, and accessibility checks into the workflow, and automate them through CI.」
