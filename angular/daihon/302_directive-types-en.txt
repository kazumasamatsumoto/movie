# #302 「Directive の3つの種類」台本

ずんだもん「What kinds of Directives are there?」
四国めたん「They fall into three categories: component, structural, and attribute Directives.」
ずんだもん「How do we use each one?」
四国めたん「Structural Directives add or remove parts of the DOM tree, attribute Directives change appearance or behavior, and component Directives have their own UI.」
ずんだもん「How should we organize them in a project?」
四国めたん「Separate them by type in directories and share a naming convention so they're easy to grasp.」
