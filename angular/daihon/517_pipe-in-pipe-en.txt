# #517 「Pipe 内で Pipe を使う？」台本

ずんだもん「Can we call a pipe from inside another pipe?」
四国めたん「Pipes are functions, so you can import one and reuse its transform logic, but chaining in the template is usually cleaner.」
ずんだもん「So composition is possible, but keep it simple.」
四国めたん「Right-prefer template chaining unless you need shared utilities.」
