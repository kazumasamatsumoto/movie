# #347 「*ngFor="let item of items"」台本

ずんだもん「How should we read `let item of items` again?」
四国めたん「It takes each element from `items` and lets you use it as `item`.」
ずんだもん「In the template we just reference it like `{{ item.name }}`, right?」
四国めたん「Exactly. Descriptive property names help everyone understand quickly.」
ずんだもん「Naming sense matters after all!」
