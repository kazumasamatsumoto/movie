# #332 「* 構文の意味」台本

ずんだもん「What does the asterisk in templates actually do?」
四国めたん「It's syntactic sugar that Angular expands into `<ng-template>` for Structural Directives.」
ずんだもん「So internally it's converted into a template tag?」
四国めたん「Exactly. Manually expanding `*ngIf` makes the mechanism clear.」
ずんだもん「For readability we usually keep using the asterisk, right.」
