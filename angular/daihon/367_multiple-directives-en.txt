# #367 「複数ディレクティブの適用」台本

ずんだもん「Can we attach multiple Structural Directives to one element?」
四国めたん「Not directly. Split them with `ng-container` and apply them in sequence.」
ずんだもん「What about Attribute Directives on the same element?」
四国めたん「Those are fine. Only Structural Directives follow the one-per-element rule.」
ずんだもん「Good to keep in mind during design.」
