# #310 「selector の指定方法」台本

ずんだもん「How should we decide a Directive's selector?」
四国めたん「Pick element, attribute, or class selectors depending on the use case and add your app-specific prefix.」
ずんだもん「Can we list multiple selectors?」
四国めたん「Yes. Separate them with commas to share the same logic under multiple attribute names.」
ずんだもん「Does the syntax change for standalone Directives?」
四国めたん「No change. Just declare it explicitly like `selector: '[appHighlight]'`.」
ずんだもん「Any tips for maintainability?」
四国めたん「Reflect the role in the name and choose concise identifiers so the impact is easy to picture.」
