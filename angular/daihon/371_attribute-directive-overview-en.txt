# #371 「Attribute Directive とは？」台本

ずんだもん「What exactly is an Attribute Directive?」
四国めたん「It's a lightweight directive you attach like an attribute to add appearance or behavior to an existing element.」
ずんだもん「How is it different from a component?」
四国めたん「It has no template—its job is simply to tweak the host element's state.」
ずんだもん「So we can encapsulate small behaviors neatly!」
