# #582 「share() オペレーターの併用」台本

ずんだもん「What if we still need multiple AsyncPipe usages?」
四国めたん「Share the subscription on the Observable with operators like `share()` or `shareReplay()` so multiple AsyncPipe calls reuse a single stream.」
ずんだもん「So RxJS handles the optimization.」
四国めたん「Yes-optimize on both the pipe and Observable sides.」
