# #616 「Pristine State - 未操作」台本

ずんだもん「What does the pristine state represent?」
四国めたん「It means the control hasn't been changed since initialization. Once the user edits it, pristine becomes false.」
ずんだもん「How do we reset it?」
四国めたん「Call reset on the control or the whole form to mark it pristine again.」
