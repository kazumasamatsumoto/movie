# #360 「[ngSwitch] での値指定」台本

ずんだもん「What kind of value should we pass to [ngSwitch]?」
四国めたん「You can pass any expression, but enums or constants make it easier to read.」
ずんだもん「Can we feed it with a Signal value?」
四国めたん「Of course. Invoke the Signal and switch cases according to the state.」
ずんだもん「Providing type-safe values is the key.」
