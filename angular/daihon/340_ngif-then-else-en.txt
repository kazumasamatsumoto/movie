# #340 「*ngIf="condition; then/else"」台本

ずんだもん「What's handy about specifying both then and else?」
四国めたん「With `*ngIf="condition; then success; else failure"`, you declare both templates in one place.」
ずんだもん「So we need to prepare two template references?」
四国めたん「Right. Give them readable names and keep the markup organized.」
ずんだもん「Even as conditions multiply, the visibility stays clear—nice!」
