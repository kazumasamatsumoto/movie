# #320 「Renderer2 - 安全なDOM操作」台本

ずんだもん「How does Renderer2 work?」
四国めたん「It's a platform-independent abstraction so you can manipulate without worrying about the DOM, SSR, or Web Workers.」
ずんだもん「Does it provide many methods?」
四国めたん「It covers the basics like styles, classes, attributes, and listener control.」
ずんだもん「How do we inject it?」
四国めたん「Declare `private readonly renderer: Renderer2` in the constructor.」
ずんだもん「How is it different from direct DOM access?」
四国めたん「Renderer2 converts calls to safe APIs internally, giving you compatibility and easier testing.」
