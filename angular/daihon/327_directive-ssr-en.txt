# #327 「SSR対応のDOM操作」台本

ずんだもん「What should Directives watch out for when supporting SSR?」
四国めたん「There's no DOM on the server, so operate conditionally.」
ずんだもん「Writing `isPlatformBrowser` everywhere sounds tedious.」
四国めたん「Create utilities that call Renderer2 only in the browser and reuse them.」
ずんだもん「What happens to event listeners during SSR?」
四国めたん「They are ignored on the server, but make sure they register safely when the browser boots.」
ずんだもん「How does it play with hydration?」
四国めたん「Minimize side effects during the first render so the markup matches what SSR produced.」
