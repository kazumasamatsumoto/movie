# #319 「直接DOM操作の注意点」台本

ずんだもん「Why is direct DOM manipulation considered risky?」
四国めたん「It brings platform differences and XSS risks, and it might crash under SSR.」
ずんだもん「So switching everything to Renderer2 keeps us safe?」
四国めたん「It fixes most issues, but sanitize user input whenever you handle it.」
ずんだもん「What about performance impact?」
四国めたん「If you mutate the DOM outside the zone it won't be detected, so combine it with ChangeDetectorRef.」
ずんだもん「What should we check during audits?」
四国めたん「Look for heavy use of `nativeElement`, direct access through external libraries, and missing SSR conditionals.」
