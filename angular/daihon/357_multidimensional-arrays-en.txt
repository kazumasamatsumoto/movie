# #357 「多次元配列の表示」台本

ずんだもん「How do we loop when we need to render a multidimensional array?」
四国めたん「Use an outer `*ngFor` for rows and an inner one for columns—that's the basic structure.」
ずんだもん「What about track keys?」
四国めたん「Provide stable keys for both rows and columns, and combine them in trackBy to minimize re-renders.」
ずんだもん「Sounds perfect for tables and grids.」
