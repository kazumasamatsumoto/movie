# #301 「Directive とは？DOM拡張の仕組み」台本

ずんだもん「What exactly does a Directive do?」
四国めたん「In Angular it lets you add behavior or display logic to the DOM without touching the template itself.」
ずんだもん「How is it different from a component?」
四国めたん「Components have their own templates and styles, while Directives are slim layers that extend the DOM.」
ずんだもん「Where does it make sense to use them?」
四国めたん「Extract reusable DOM logic—like loops or state-driven style switches—into Directives.」
