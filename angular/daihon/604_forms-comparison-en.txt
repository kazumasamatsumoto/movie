# #604 「Template-driven vs Reactive 比較」台本

ずんだもん「What's the major difference between template-driven and reactive forms?」
四国めたん「The way you write code, how easy it is to test, and the granularity of state management all change.」
ずんだもん「The development experience must feel very different.」
四国めたん「Yes—template-driven is intuitive, reactive offers tighter control.」
ずんだもん「So the team's skill set matters when choosing.」
四国めたん「Exactly—evaluate the project size and each member's strengths before deciding.」
