# #297 「Avatar Component - アバター表示」台本

ずんだもん「What should an Avatar Component include?」
四国めたん「Provide fallbacks for an image, initials, and an icon, and let an Input switch the size and shape.」
ずんだもん「What if the image fails to load?」
四国めたん「Update a signal in the onerror handler and switch over to the initials.」
ずんだもん「How about accessibility?」
四国めたん「Convey the meaning with alt text and aria-label, and if it's purely decorative mark it aria-hidden to avoid narration.」
