# #561 「Pure vs Impure の使い分け」台本

ずんだもん「How should we choose between pure and impure pipes?」
四国めたん「Stick with pure pipes by default and switch to impure only when you must react to mutable data or external state.」
ずんだもん「So impure pipes carry risk.」
四国めたん「Exactly-consider the performance cost before opting in.」
