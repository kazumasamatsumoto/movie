# #318 「nativeElement の使用」台本

ずんだもん「What should we watch for when we touch nativeElement?」
四国めたん「It tends to be browser-dependent, and under SSR or Web Workers it might not exist.」
ずんだもん「But it's faster performance-wise sometimes, right?」
四国めたん「If you really need it, check that it exists before operating on it.」
ずんだもん「How do we cast it safely?」
四国めたん「Write it explicitly like `const el = this.el.nativeElement as HTMLElement;`.」
ずんだもん「Is Renderer2 the alternative?」
四国めたん「Exactly. Operate through Renderer2 whenever possible to keep compatibility.」
