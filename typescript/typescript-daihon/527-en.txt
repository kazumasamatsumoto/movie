ずんだもん「What does filtering mean at the type level?」
四国めたん「We use conditional types plus `never` to strip away types that don't match a condition.」
ずんだもん「What pattern should I follow?」
四国めたん「`T extends Condition ? T : never` keeps only the types that satisfy `Condition`.」
ずんだもん「So it's like filtering an array, but for types!」
四国めたん「It's the basis of helpers like `Exclude`, `Extract`, and `NonNullable`.」
ずんだもん「Type-level filtering unlocks so much reuse!」
