ずんだもん「How do you use it?」
四国めたん「Write `user?.name`; if `user` is `null`, it yields `undefined`, otherwise you get the `name`.」
ずんだもん「Got it! That's safe access!」
四国めたん「It even works on nested properties—`user?.address?.city` chains naturally.」
