四国めたん「Null is an explicit empty value, while undefined means 'not set'. They convey different intent.」
ずんだもん「How do we pick one?」
四国めたん「Use null for intentional emptiness; use undefined for optional properties or missing returns. strictNullChecks enforces the distinction.」
ずんだもん「Understood—the meaning differs.」
四国めたん「typeof helps too: null yields 'object'; undefined yields 'undefined'.」
